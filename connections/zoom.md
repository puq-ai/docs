# Zoom
Learn how to connect Zoom to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Go to the Zoom App Marketplace:  
https://marketplace.zoom.us/

## Step 2
From the top bar, open the **Develop** dropdown and select **Build App**.

## Step 3
Select **General App** as the app type and click **Create**.

## Step 4
After clicking Create, you will be redirected to the app configuration page.  
If you are not redirected automatically, return to the homepage, click **Manage** from the top bar, find your project name in the list, and click it.

## Step 5
Under **App Credentials**, you can find the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.

![Zoom]({{ '/assets/images/connections/zoom/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Zoom integration form in **puq.ai**.
