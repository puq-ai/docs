# Callendy
Learn how to connect to Callendly in puq.ai.

## Option 1
If you logged into puq.ai with Gmail, you can use the Gmail account I used to log in (Use Default (puq.ai)).

![Callendly]({{ '/assets/images/connections/callendly/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Option 2
You can connect your Callendly account.

### Step 1
Click **Integrations & apps**.

### Step 2
Click **Api and webhooks**.

![Callendly]({{ '/assets/images/connections/callendly/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

### Step 3
On the screen that opens, click **My Apps** from the topbar.

### Step 4
Click **Create new app**.

### Step 5 
Fill the form
![Callendly]({{ '/assets/images/connections/callendly/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }
Click **Next**

### Step 6
On screen that opens, you can see your **Client ID** and **Client secret**.
Use this information to fill the form in puq.ai.
![Callendly]({{ '/assets/images/connections/callendly/000003.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }
