# Constant Contact
Learn how to connect to Constant Contact in puq.ai

## Step 1
Go to Application Page from developer console or use this url: 'https://app.constantcontact.com/pages/dma/portal/'.

## Step 2
Click **New Application**

![Constant Contact]({{ '/assets/images/connections/constant-contact/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 3 
Fill the form.

![Constant Contact]({{ '/assets/images/connections/constant-contact/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="60%" }

## Step 4
Click **Edit** on your new application.

## Step 5
Fill the Redirect URI field with 'https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback'

![Constant Contact]({{ '/assets/images/connections/constant-contact/000003.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 6
Click **Save** button.

## Step 7
know you can see your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** into your new application's edit page or information page.
