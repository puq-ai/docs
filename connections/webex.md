# Webex
Learn how to connect Webex to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Go to the Webex for Developers portal:  
https://developer.webex.com/

## Step 2
On the homepage, click **Start Building Apps**.

## Step 3
Click **Create an Integration**.

![Webex]({{ '/assets/images/connections/webex/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 4
Fill in the integration form.  
In the **Redirect URI** field, enter the following value: 'https://app.puq.ai/oauth/callback'

Select the necessary **Scopes**.

## Step 5
At the bottom of the page, click **Add Integration**.

## Step 6
Click your **user profile icon** in the top bar and select **My Webex Apps** from the dropdown menu.

## Step 7
Click the app you just created.

## Step 8
On the application details page, you can find the **Client ID** at the top.  
You can generate a **Client Secret** by clicking **Regenerate the client secret**.

![Webex]({{ '/assets/images/connections/webex/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Webex integration form in **puq.ai**.
