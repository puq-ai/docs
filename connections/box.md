# Box
Learn how to connect to Box in puq.ai

## Step 1
Login to Box app.

## Step 2
Click on the **Developer Console** bottom of the sidebar.

## Step 3
Fill the form.
Choose the application Type 'Web App'
## Step 4
Click **Create App**

## Step 5
Fill the redirect url with 'https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback'

## Step 6
Under OAuth 2.0 Credentials, you can find Client ID and Client Secret information.
