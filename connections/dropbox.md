# Dropbox
Learn how to connect to Dropbox in puq.ai

## Step 1
Go to Dropbox Developer and login.

## Step 2
Click **App Console** from topbar.

## Step 3
click **Create app**

## Step 4
Fill the form.

![Dropbox]({{ '/assets/images/connections/dropbox/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 5
Click **Create app**

## Step 6 
If your new application's page is opened continue with step 7,
else return back to App Console page and click your new application.

## Step 7
Set the Redirect URIs with 'https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback/'

![Dropbox]({{ '/assets/images/connections/dropbox/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 8
You can see your **App key** and **App secret**.
Use this information to fill the form in puq.ai.
