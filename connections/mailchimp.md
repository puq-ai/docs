# Mailchimp
Learn how to connect Mailchimp to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Sign in to your Mailchimp account.

## Step 2
Click your profile picture in the top bar.

## Step 3
From the top menu, click Extras, then select Registered apps from the dropdown.

![Mailchimp]({{ '/assets/images/connections/mailchimp/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 4
Click Register an app.

![Mailchimp]({{ '/assets/images/connections/mailchimp/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 5
After completing the registration, Mailchimp will redirect you to the app details page.
On this page, you can find the basic OAuth information for your application, including **Client ID** and **Client secret**

![Mailchimp]({{ '/assets/images/connections/mailchimp/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Mailchimp integration form in puq.ai.
