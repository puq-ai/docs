# Notion
Learn how to connect Notion to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Go to the Nitoin app. 
Go to profile.
Go to integrations.

## Step 2
Click New Integration.

## Step 3
Fill in the integration form.
Make sure to add the following value to the Redirect URL field: https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback/

![Notion]({{ '/assets/images/connections/notion/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="40%" }

## Step 4
Click Save.

## Step 5
Open the integration you just created.
On the integration details page, you will find the Client ID and Client Secret.

![Notion]({{ '/assets/images/connections/notion/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Notion integration form in puq.ai.
