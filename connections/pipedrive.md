# Pipedrive
Learn how to connect Pipedrive to puq.ai.

## Step 1

Go to:
https://developers.pipedrive.com/

Sign in to your Pipedrive account.

## Step 2
Click your user profile in the top bar and select Developer Hub from the dropdown menu.

## Step 3
On the Developer Hub page, you will see two tabs: Public apps and Private apps.
Choose whether you want to create a public app or a private app based on your needs.

## Step 4
Click Create public app or Create private app.

## Step 5
Fill in the application form.
In the Redirect URI field, enter the following value:

https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback

## Step 6
Click Save.

## Step 7
Return to the Developer Hub and click the app you just created.

## Step 8
From the bottom-right section, click OAuth & access scopes.

## Step 9
In the modal that opens, you will find the Client ID and Client Secret at the bottom.

![Pipedrive]({{ '/assets/images/connections/pipedrive/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Pipedrive integration form in puq.ai.
