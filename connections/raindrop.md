# Raindrop
Learn how to connect Raindrop to puq.ai.

## Step 1
After signing in to **Raindrop**, click on your **user profile** in the sidebar and select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.

## Step 2
From the sidebar, click **Integrations**.

## Step 3
Click **Create new application**.

![Raindrop]({{ '/assets/images/connections/raindrop/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 4
Fill in the form and click **Create**.

![Raindrop]({{ '/assets/images/connections/raindrop/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 5
Click the application you just created.

![Raindrop]({{ '/assets/images/connections/raindrop/000003.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 6
On the application details page, you will find the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.

![Raindrop]({{ '/assets/images/connections/raindrop/000004.png' | relative_url }}){: width="60%" }

Use this information to complete the Raindrop integration form in **puq.ai**.
