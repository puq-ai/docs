# Salesforce
Learn how to connect Salesforce to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Sign in to your **Salesforce** account.

## Step 2
Click the **gear icon** in the top-right corner and select **Quick Advanced Setup**.

## Step 3
On the new screen, use the **Search Setup** field in the top bar and search for **App Manager**.

![Salesforce]({{ '/assets/images/connections/salesforce/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 4
On the App Manager screen, click **New External App**.

## Step 5
Fill in the form.  
Complete the **API** section as specified.

## Step 6
Click **Create**.

## Step 7
Using the search field, search for **External Client App Manager**.

## Step 8
Click the app you just created.

## Step 9
On the app details page, scroll down and click **Settings**.

## Step 10
Open **OAuth Settings** and click **Consumer Key and Secret**.

## Step 11
Here, you will find the **Client Key** and **Client Secret**.

![Salesforce]({{ '/assets/images/connections/salesforce/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Salesforce integration form in **puq.ai**.
