# Pushbullet
Learn how to connect Pushbullet to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Sign in to your Pushbullet account.

## Step 2
Go to the homepage.
(You can do this by clicking the logo in the top bar.)

## Step 3
From the sidebar, click Settings, then select Clients.

![Pushbullet]({{ '/assets/images/connections/pushbullet/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

## Step 4
Fill in the form.
In the Redirect URL field, enter the following value:

https://app.puq.ai/oauth2/callback

## Step 5
Click Add A New OAuth Client.

## Step 6

The newly created client will appear on the same page.
At the top of the client card, you will find the Client ID and Client Secret.

![Pushbullet]({{ '/assets/images/connections/pushbullet/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the Pushbullet integration form in puq.ai.
