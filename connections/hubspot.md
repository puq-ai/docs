# HubSpot
Learn how to connect HubSpot to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Sign in to your HubSpot account.

## Step 2
From the sidebar, scroll to the bottom and click Developer.

## Step 3
From the sidebar, click Legacy Apps.

## Step 4
Click Create Legacy App.

## Step 5
Open the Auth tab and enter the following Redirect URL: 'https://app.puq.ai/oauth/callback'

## Step 6
Complete the remaining fields in the form.

## Step 7
Click Create App.

## Step 8
After the app is created, you will find the Client ID and Client Secret on the app details page.

![HubSpot]({{ '/assets/images/connections/hubspot/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="100%" }

Use this information to complete the HubSpot integration form in puq.ai.
