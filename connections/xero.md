# Xero
Learn how to connect Xero to puq.ai.

## Step 1
Go to the Xero Developer Platform:  
https://developer.xero.com/

## Step 2
From the top bar, click **My Apps**, then click **New App**.

## Step 3
Fill in the application form.

![Xero]({{ '/assets/images/connections/xero/000001.png' | relative_url }}){: width="40%" }

## Step 4
Click **Create App**.

## Step 5
The app details page will open.  
From the sidebar, click **Configuration**.

## Step 6
On the Configuration page, you will find the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.

![Xero]({{ '/assets/images/connections/xero/000002.png' | relative_url }}){: width="60%" }

Use this information to complete the Xero integration form in **puq.ai**.
